Methodology

This project compares Dominican and Mexican Spanish through analysis of Reddit posts, focusing on pronoun usage, slang, and other linguistic features. Data was collected, cleaned, and visualized to uncover patterns across the two dialects.

Data Analysis

Using Python, we processed the text to count occurrences of key pronouns and slang terms. Charts were generated to compare frequencies and patterns between Dominican and Mexican Spanish.

Example visualizations include side-by-side bar charts, pie charts of slang usage, and pronoun distribution comparisons.

Results

The analysis shows noticeable differences in pronoun usage, with Dominican Spanish favoring more overt pronouns. Certain slang terms are more prevalent in each dialect, reflecting regional and stylistic variations.

Interpretation

These patterns likely emerge from a combination of:

  • Dialectal conventions and social norms
  • Translation or transcription choices in Reddit posts
  • Stylistic differences among users

Next Steps

Future work could include a deeper exploration of gendered pronoun usage, slang evolution, and extending the dataset to include additional Latin American dialects. Visualizations can be made interactive for web display.
